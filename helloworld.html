About Tech Kro-Home Study Module

What is Home Study Module?

It is a 8-day program that teaches you very basic programming to help you develop the right skills for the Tech Karo program. You will be given videos to watch and after each video you will have to take a short quiz. At the end of the 8-day program, you will have to submit a short technical project. If you successfully complete the 10-day home study module, you have to give the Math, English, Problem Solving & Computer Test, and if you clear the test you then you will have to give an interview to Tech Karo team.
